Where we gather

The Rialto
Theater

Our ceremony and reception will both take place at the historic Rialto Theater, a beautifully restored space rooted in Tampa's cultural past. We loved the idea of celebrating in a place with history — where stories have unfolded for generations — and beginning our own chapter in a space designed for gathering and connection.

The venue is indoors and climate-controlled. Complimentary parking is available across the street in the public lot.
